About Us

Welcome to Lifelong Learning Society: Empowering Minds, Enriching Lives



Lifelong Learning Society is dedicated to fostering a culture of continuous education,
personal growth, and skill development for individuals of all ages and backgrounds.
Our mission is to promote lifelong learning as a fundamental human right,
providing accessible and inclusive educational opportunities that enable individuals to reach their full potential.
From diverse learning programs to skill development initiatives and community engagement opportunities,
we strive to empower minds and enrich lives, creating a brighter future for all.

LIFELONG LEARNING SOCIETY

LIFELONG LEARNING SOCIETY (LLS) IS A NON-GOVERNMENTAL ORGANIZATION COMMITTED TO PROMOTING LIFELONG LEARNING OPPORTUNITIES FOR INDIVIDUALS OF ALL AGES WITHIN GARISSA COUNTY.

Community Engagement


Join our vibrant community of lifelong learners and gain access to exclusive benefits, discounts, and events by becoming a member. Volunteer your time and expertise to support our educational programs and initiatives, or make a tax-deductible donation to help us expand access to lifelong learning opportunities for all individuals. Collaborate with Lifelong Learning Society to develop and implement innovative educational programs, research projects, and advocacy campaigns, and together, we can make lifelong learning a reality for all.




Stay Connected


Stay connected with us on social media and subscribe to our newsletter to stay up-to-date with the latest news, events, and opportunities from Lifelong Learning Society. Join us on our journey to empower minds, enrich lives, and create a world where learning knows no limits. Together, we can make a difference and build a brighter future for generations to come.
