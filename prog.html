Our Programs

The LLS is a non-profit organization dedicated to advancing education and skills development beyond traditional schooling. We offer a diverse range of programs and activities tailored to meet the needs and interests of our members.

Adult Education

Courses: Varied curriculum including literacy, numeracy, computer skills, and vocational training. Schedule: Flexible timings to accommodate working adults. Instructors: Experienced educators committed to adult learning.

Youth Empowerment

Workshops: Leadership training, career guidance, and soft skills development. Mentorship: Connecting youth with mentors in various professions. Activities: Sports, arts, and cultural events for holistic development.

Donation

Volunteering to share your expertise as a workshop facilitator or mentor is a valuable way to contribute to your community and make a meaningful impact on individuals seeking to learn and grow.

Community Outreach

Partnerships: Collaborations with local schools, businesses, and government agencies. Events: Seminars, conferences, and awareness campaigns on lifelong learning.

Volunteer
