LIFELONG LEARNING SOCIETY

LIFELONG LEARNING SOCIETY (LLS) IS A NON-GOVERNMENTAL ORGANIZATION COMMITTED TO PROMOTING LIFELONG LEARNING OPPORTUNITIES FOR INDIVIDUALS OF ALL AGES WITHIN GARISSA COUNTY.

Our Mission, Vission and Objectives

Mission

Our mission is to provide accessible and lifelong learning opportunities to all individuals.

Vision

We envision a world where everyone has the opportunity to continuously learn, grow, and thrive.

Objectives
  • Empower individuals through education
  • Promote lifelong learning culture
  • Facilitate skill development
